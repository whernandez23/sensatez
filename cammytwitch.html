Cammy's Upcoming, Twitch-Themed Color Variant Surprisingly Isn't Free, Sorry

by William Hernandez August 30, 2024


Yesterday, Capcom announced that Cammy would be getting an exclusive, Twitch-themed color for her Outfit 2. Street Fighter 6 has delved into the realm of Twitch drops before, but this new collaboration takes it a step further with this special, re-color offering. On X, the Street Fighter account made it seem quite easy to get, but their post is a tad bit deceptive.



Reading The Fine Print — The Costume Costs Money


While the Street Fighter team's post on X might make this promotion seem all-inclusive, unfortunately, this color variant for Cammy isn't available to just any "Twitch viewers." To get a code for the costume color, you need to gift or buy a Twitch subscription (of any tier) to a streamer within the Street Fighter 6 category. This is a requirement — there's no getting around it. Prime Subs are excluded so you have to pay out of pocket.

There's nothing really wrong with locking new costumes behind payments — it's what people are used to after all — but it's really weird to not be up-front about it. This is basically a $6 USD costume; it should be advertised as such. Of course, it has the added benefit of supporting a content creator so it's a little different from the norm. Still, it's really strange that Capcom chose to be so vague with their language.

The Twitch-exclusive Cammy color will only be obtainable between September 2, 2024 and September 30, 2024. It's important to not forget that!


While Prime Subs might not get you this Cammy color, Amazon Prime is still a great deal. Amazon Prime Student is rebranding to Amazon Prime for Young Adults and, now, new members will be able to start a 6-month trial for $0. If you're within the ages of 18-24 or are a student, this is a must-get.

Also, keep your eyes peeled for our X account as we will be giving this Cammy color away at some point this upcoming September! All follows will be greatly appreciated — don't miss out on an opportunity to get this exclusive color for free!
