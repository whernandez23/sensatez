Metroid Prime 4: Beyond Goes Beyond With New Psychic Powers

by William Hernandez March 27, 2025


Not much was expected out of the last Nintendo Direct of the Nintendo Switch era, so a new look at Metroid Prime 4: Beyond was definitely a pleasant surprise. Nintendo wasn't all that shy with the details this time around, revealing Metroid Prime 4's new core gameplay element: psychic abilities. Innovation is an expectation for Nintendo, and Metroid Prime 4: Beyond is set to offer a unique experience within the franchise.

Planet Viewros, The Home Of Psychics

Metroid Prime 4: Beyond takes place on Planet Viewros, a mysterious planet that Samus was unexpectedly transported to. As tends to be the case in most Metroid games, Viewros is filled to the brim with hostile alien life. It's not all bad, though, as the planet also seems to be the home of a psychically-gifted alien race. And to them, Samus serves a greater purpose. She is the Chosen One.

Plenty Of Psychic Abilities In Metroid Prime 4: Beyond

Samus is eventually granted psychic abilities of her own in Metroid Prime 4: Beyond, allowing her to take on the world around her in completely new ways. Her primary power is that of telekinesis, which she can use to operate mechanisms from afar. Samus is also capable of using her abilities offensively, redirecting the trajectory of her beams using just her mind. In gameplay, that translates to taking control of the beam after it has been fired.
