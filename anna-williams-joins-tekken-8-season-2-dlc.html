Anna Williams Joins Tekken 8 As The First Season 2 DLC Character

by William Hernandez February 24, 2025


Tekken 8 still has a lot left to give after the end of just its first season, and the action is about to heat up with the long-awaited arrival of Anna Williams to the roster. Revealed in the most recent TEKKEN Talk, Anna's release is set to kick off Season 2 of the game in style. As paid DLC, of course. The Williams sisters only work for money, after all.

Anna Williams Headlines The Second Wave Of DLC

Tekken 8 has big plans for Season 2. Major changes are coming to the game. But what will matter most to a lot of casual fans is the next wave of DLC. Anna Williams is leading the charge there, releasing alongside the big Season 2 update on March 31, 2025 (PDT). That's only for early access, though, to be clear. Anna officially releases for all players on April 3, 2025 (PDT).

Anna Williams will be coming with a revamped set of moves in Tekken 8. Her new rocket launcher, Lovely Tom, has given her moveset a whole new gimmick to work around. Anna appears to debuff opponents now with some moves. There will surely be a lot to explore there. Of course, Anna still comes with classic moves, like her Heel Grind.

A Whole New Look For Anna Williams

Anna Williams has always been fond of bob cuts, but this new look for her is quite the departure from her past designs. Anna's making quite the beauty statement with her diagonally angled bob cut that just perfectly hides her left eye. As if that weren't enough, she has red highlights now to really cement her love for the color. Anna's outfit, at the very least, is fairly standard for her, going for something of a gothic design. She is wearing pants now, though. That's actually a first.
