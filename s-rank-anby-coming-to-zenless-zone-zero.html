An S-Rank Anby Variant Is Coming To Zenless Zone Zero Version 1.6

by William Hernandez January 23, 2025


As one of the first playable agents in Zenless Zone Zero, Anby Demara undoubtedly holds a special place in many players' hearts. And now, it appears she will be taking the next step in her journey, suiting up for something that is surely important. The new Silver Soldier - Anby in Zenless Zone Zero Version 1.6 will redefine what the character is capable of.



Here's What We Know About Silver Soldier - Anby So Far

Silver Soldier - Anby will be an S-Rank Electric Attack character. This clearly won't just be a new skin for Anby, given that she is normally a Stun character. To add to that, this version of Anby is definitely going to come with a whole new playstyle. Her holding two swords probably isn't just for show.

As for why we're getting a new Anby, that hasn't been revealed yet. She is determined by something, considering that she doesn't want Nicole to know what she's doing. We just don't know what those specific motivations are. This Anby being of an unknown faction is also pretty interesting.

Marketing-wise, this is also simply a smart move from the Zenless Zone Zero team. Anby is one of the most recognizable characters in the game. Even people who haven't played ZZZ have probably seen her before. Adding more to her character is going to catch the attention of many. That's how you retain a strong audience.


Another New Character Being Added: Pulchra

Pulchra Fellini will be an A-Rank Physical Stun character, the first to fill that specific role. She is of the Sons of Calydon faction now. Burnice apparently had quite the strong influence on her joining.
